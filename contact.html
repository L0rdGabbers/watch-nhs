Outreach/Education

Together with the Bristol and Cardiff PICUs, WATCh provides outreach and education through a series of roadshows and feedback events.

A significant part of the WATCh team role is to provide a formal Outreach Education Programme for hospitals within the region who are involved in the care of the critically ill or injured child. We are committed to providing at least one session in each hospital per year.

Typical Outreach Education:

  • Review of clinical cases presenting to your hospital
  • Update management of the critically ill or injured child
  • Simulation and case-based discussions
  • Educational and interactive lectures
